  <h2>Manifesto</h2>

  <p><strong>6am — world, at six</strong> was born out of a simple need: starting the day with a clear, concise, and essential overview of global events, free from the noise of modern media.</p>

  <p>Every morning at 6:00 AM, we publish a brief digest highlighting the day’s most relevant international news. No clickbait, no sensationalism—just the facts you need to stay informed in a matter of minutes.</p>

  <hr style="border: none; border-top: 1px solid #e1e1e1; margin: 2rem 0;">

  <h2>The Web as It Should Be</h2>

  <p>We believe in an open, respectful, and distraction-free web. That is why <strong>6am</strong> is built on three core principles:</p>

  <ul>
    <li><strong>Zero Ads:</strong> No banners, popups, or sponsored content interrupting your reading experience.</li>
    <li><strong>Zero Cookies & Tracking:</strong> We do not track readers, use invasive analytics, or collect personal data.</li>
    <li><strong>Completely Free:</strong> Quality information should remain accessible to everyone. Ever.</li>
  </ul>

  <hr style="border: none; border-top: 1px solid #e1e1e1; margin: 2rem 0;">
